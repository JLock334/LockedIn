About Us

LockedIn Photography specializes in capturing timeless moments with precision and creativity. Founded in 2008 by James Lockhart, an award-winning photographer with a passion for storytelling through the lens. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional photography services tailored to our clients' unique needs.



Services Offered: Weddings & Events: From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, we capture every special moment. Portraits: Professional and personal portraits that reflect individuality and character. Commercial Photography: High-quality images for advertising, marketing, and corporate events. Photo Editing & Retouching: Enhance and perfect images to meet the highest standards.



Mission Statement: At LockedIn Photography, our mission is to exceed client expectations by delivering stunning imagery that captures emotions and memories, ensuring every moment is beautifully preserved.
